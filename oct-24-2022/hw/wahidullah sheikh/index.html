Lionel Messi


The 2022 World Cup in Qatar next month will be
the last of Lionel Messi's storied career.
The 35-year-old Argentina star,
who will play in his fifth World Cup,
is still seeking his first title at the showpiece tournament
(which begins on Nov. 20 this year) and admitted
he feels nervous ahead of his
last appearance on world football's biggest stage.
